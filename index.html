AI art is ugly and annoying. If you have been an artist for a long time or often watch videos by SamDoesArt, then you already know exactly why it sucks.

AI "art" is bad because it steals bits of other artists work without thier consent and stuffs them all together like a mad scientist.

Real art is meant to take time, and actualy has meaning behind it.

How NOT to find AI

Firstly you need to know what AI art looks like. It often has lots of unneccesary details, and practicaly only uses very saturated colours.

But a major giveaway is the hands. AI just cant understand what a hand is, they often have to many fingers or just on look quite right

Now you know how to see AI lets talk aout what you can do to avoid it.

Places that already have features like this

If you do art a lot, you have likely gone on pinterest to find references, I do.

An incredibly useful feature is the ability to turn off AI images

To do this, go into profile settings > refine recomendations > AI content > turn everything off
